OCCRP works with teams of regional journalists on cross-border investigative stories both in broadcast and print formats. It does not hire full-time journalists. Reporters must have citizenship from Bosnia and Herzegovina, Serbia, Kosovo, Macedonia, Montenegro, Albania, Russia, Ukraine, Moldova, Belarus, Romania, Hungary, Slovakia, the Czech Republic, Croatia, Slovenia, Poland, Turkey, the Baltics, a Caucasus country, or a former CIS Central Asian country. Those interested in working on an OCCRP story send a CV and links to your published stories to drew (at) occrp.org.
